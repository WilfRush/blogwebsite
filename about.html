About

What is The Golf Think Tank?

The Golf Think Tank is a golf blog that is designed to create more discussion around the mental side of the game.

Aswell as blogs about golf psychology, there are tips & tricks, equipment reviews and news about the world of golf.

We want to provide value to the reader, so that they can take this value and use it to improve there own game

How can this blog help you?

The Golf Think Tank is made for golfers of all abilities From the 28 handicapper to the aspiring tour players.

The mental side of golf is what this blog will focus on, as I believe it is such an important part of golf. As Jack Nicklaus: arguably the greatest golfer of all time once said; "Golf is 80% mental, 10% ablity and 10% luck". So with such an big of the game being in the head, the psychological aspect should not be overlooked when learning the game.

What is the aim of this blog?

The aim of this blog is to educate golfers on the mental side of golf, and how they can use this to improve their own game and ultimately shoots better scores.

Also this blog aims to keep the golfer updated on the world of golf, with tournement results, latest news and equipment reviews.
